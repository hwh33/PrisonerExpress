Donate to Prisoner Express

Your support helps to keep our many prisoner programs going. For donations of...

  • $15.00, you will receive your choice of a poetry book.
  • $25.00 or more, you will receive both of the poetry books shown below.
  • $35.00 or more you will receive both books and a set of postcards created from prisoners' envelope art.

The library is a tax exempt organization. Anyone receiving books or cards for the stated donations cannot claim the donation for an income tax deduction. Donations that are not given in exchange for gifts are tax deductible. Any amount in addition to the $35.00 is tax deductible.

You can use paypal below to make your donations or you can print out our Donation form and send a check made payable to Alternatives Library. Checks can be sent to the address at the bottom of the page.
